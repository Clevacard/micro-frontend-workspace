The Liverpool ONE Gift Card is the perfect gift for every occasion, from Christmas and birthdays to graduations, and more.

Redeemable at majority of our 170 shops, cafes, bars, restaurants and entertainment venues, lucky recipients can treat themselves to whatever they like, at their own convenience – be it new clothes, a meal for two or even a trip to the cinema. Simply load it up with £10 to £400 and your Gift Card is ready to go!

Liverpool ONE Gift Cards can be purchased from our friendly team at the Tourist Information Centre on Wall Street.

Please note: Liverpool ONE Gift Cards cannot be used online and expire 12 months after the date of purchase.
